ResourceReaper maintains the TCP connection to Ryuk.

ResourceReaper.GetAndStartNewAsync(DotNet.Testcontainers.Configurations.IDockerEndpointAuthenticationConfiguration, string, System.TimeSpan, System.Threading.CancellationToken) will complete now.

ConnectionTerminated 

The connection to Ryuk has been terminated and Ryuk is going to clean up all associated Docker resources.
